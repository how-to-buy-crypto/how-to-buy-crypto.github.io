How to Buy Cosmos (ATOM)

A Step-by-Step-Guide on how to buy Cosmos (ATOM)

Step 1 – Signup to Kraken

You’ll first need to purchase one of the top-tier coins. Exchanges commonly accept either ETH (Ethereum) or BTC (Bitcoin) in exchange for any other Cryptocurrency. Click the button below for registration and to get your ETH (Ethereum) or BTC (Bitcoin).

Kraken Signup
Step 2 – Transmit Your Bitcoin or Ethereum to an Exchange

Your BTC (Bitcoin) or ETH (Ethereum) will need to be sent over to an Exchange now. All exchanges have a deposit form that is identical. Cosmos (ATOM) is available on Binance, so we’re going to use that as an example. Unsure on how to send Coinbase funds to an Exchange? (Just follow these easy steps)

Make sure you’ve selected the precise coin you wish to deposit.

Bitcoin must be sent to a Bitcoin deposit address, ETH to an ETH deposit address, etc. In the case you are working with a decentralized exchange, like Airswap or DDEX could be different (Just follow our how-to).

Binance Signup
Step 3 – Find Cosmos (ATOM)

As soon as your deposit has been approved, you will directly have access to your assets and you can launch buying and trading. To buy Cosmos (ATOM) on Binance, you will want to go to the ”Exchange” tab and then click on “Basic”.

To locate Cosmos (ATOM), you will need to insert the cointype (ATOM) into the search box, which is highlighted in the graphic. Make sure you have selected the proper pairing, we have highlighted this also. Click on “ATOM/BTC” to go on.

Step 4 – Purchase Cosmos (ATOM)

As soon as you have found Cosmos (ATOM), you’ll be given a Price Chart (in the Center of the image) and a few Order Books (on the Left of the image). For now we’re going to create our own Limit Order.

The ”Price” field will be filled automatically with the recent rate Cosmos (ATOM) was sold for. This can be changed to your favoured price. The field with the “Amount” tag is the volumen of ATOM you wish to purchase. The field with the “Total” tag is the whole price in BTC that you’ll settle for the complete volumen of ATOM.

If you are glad, click on “Buy ATOM”. Your order will be filled as soon as a Vendor trades their ATOM for the price you gave.

Step 5 – Withdraw Cosmos (ATOM)

Unless you are frequently trading, best advise is not to keep coins on an Cryptocurrency Exchange for an extensive period of time. In order to take off your Cosmos (ATOM), go to the tab called “Funds” and click on “Withdraw”. Cosmos (ATOM) can be safely stored using a Wallet, for instance MyEtherWallet. (MyEtherWallet Setup Guide)

Find Cosmos (ATOM) by using the provided drop-down box. Put into the quantity of Cosmos (ATOM) you’d wish to take off. Take into account the displayed minimus/fees. Also make sure you have entered your precise withdraw address.

When you are glad, make a mouse click on “Submit”. You could be requested for phone/email verification ahead of a take off.

Congratulations! You’ve successfully bought and stored Cosmos (ATOM).

That’s it!

Take offs from Crypto Trader can take anywhere from 1 – 30 minutes (rely on usage of the network). As soon as you’ve confirmed your take off with Etherscan.io or Ethplorer.io and the Cosmos (ATOM) is stored in your wallet, you are finished.
