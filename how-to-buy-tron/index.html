How to Buy TRON (TRX)

A Step-by-Step-Guide on how to buy TRON (TRX)

Step 1 – Signup to Kraken

You will first need to purchase one of the bigger currencies. Exchanges usually accept either BTC (Bitcoin) or ETH (Ethereum) in exchange for alternative Coins. Click below to register and receive your BTC (Bitcoin) or ETH (Ethereum).

Kraken Signup
Step 2 – Transfer Your ETH (Ethereum) or BTC (Bitcoin) to an Crypto Trader

Your ETH (Ethereum) or BTC (Bitcoin) has to be sent over to an Crypto Trader now. Every exchange has a deposit form that is alike. TRON (TRX) is accessible on Binance, so we’re going to use that as an sample. Not sure on how to send funds from Coinbase to an Crypto Trader? (Follow this how-to)

Make sure you have selected the correct coin you wish to deposit.

Bitcoin must be sent to a Bitcoin deposit address, Ethereum to an Ethereum deposit address, and so on. In the case you’re working with a decentralized exchange, like Airswap or DDEX could be opposed (Follow our manual).

Binance Signup
Step 3 – Locate TRON (TRX)

Once your deposit has been verified, you’ll now have access to your assets and you can start invest and trading. To buy TRON (TRX) on Binance, you’ll want to go to the tab which is called “Exchange” and mouse click on “Basic”.

In order to spot TRON (TRX), you’ll need to insert the shortname of the coin (TRX) into the search box, which is highlighted in the image. Be certain of having choosen the proper pairing, we’ve highlighted this also. Click on “TRX/BTC” to go on.

Step 4 – Buy TRON (TRX)

Once you’ve found TRON (TRX), you’ll be given a Price Chart (in the Center of the picture) and a few Order Books (Leftside of the picture). For now we are going to make our own Limit Order.

The field with the “Price” tag will be filled with the up-to-date rate TRON (TRX) was sold for. This can be changed to your favoured price. The field with the “Amount” tag is the quantity of TRX you like to buy. The field with the “Total” tag is the aggregated price in Bitcoin that you’ll settle for the complete quantity of TRX.

If you’re satisfied, click on “Buy TRX”. Your order will be filled as soon as a Vendor trades their TRX for the price you introduced.

Step 5 – Withdraw TRON (TRX)

Unless you’re regularly trading, best advise is not to keep your crypto coins on an Cryptocurrency Exchange for an longer period of time. In order to withdraw your TRON (TRX), go to the tab called “Funds” and mouse click on “Withdraw”. TRON (TRX) can be safely stored using a Wallet, e.g. MyEtherWallet. (MyEtherWallet Setup Guide)

Find TRON (TRX) by using the provided drop-down box. Enter the volumen of TRON (TRX) you’d wish to withdraw. Take into account the shown fees/minimums. Also make sure you have filled your right withdraw address.

When you’re glad, click on “Submit”. You may be requested for phone/email verification before a withdrawal.

Congrats! You’ve successfully purchased and safed TRON (TRX).

That’s it!

Take offs from Crypto Trader can take between 1 – 30 minutes (depending on network usage). As soon as you have proved your withdrawal using Ethplorer.io or Etherscan.io and the TRON (TRX) is stored within your wallet, you’re all done.
