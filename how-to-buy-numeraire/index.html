How to Buy Numeraire (NMR)

A Step-by-Step-Guide on how to buy Numeraire (NMR)

Step 1 – Join Kraken

You’ll first need to purchase one of the top-tier coins. Exchanges normally accept either Bitcoin or Ethereum in exchange for Altcoins. Click below to register and to get your Bitcoin or Ethereum.

Kraken Signup
Step 2 – Transmit Your Funds to an Crypto Exchange

Your Ethereum or Bitcoin will need to be sent over to an Crypto Exchange now. Every exchange has a deposit form that is alike. Numeraire (NMR) is obtainable on Binance, so we’re going to use that as an sample. Unsure on how to send funds from Coinbase to an Crypto Exchange? (Just follow this how-to)

Make sure you’ve chosen the right coin you would like to deposit.

Bitcoin must be sent to a Bitcoin deposit address, ETH to an ETH deposit address, and so forth. If you are using a decentralized exchange, such as Airswap or DDEX could be opposed (Just follow our manual).

Binance Signup
Step 3 – Locate Numeraire (NMR)

Once your deposit has been verified, you will directly have access to your assets and you can initiate purchasing and trading. To invest in Numeraire (NMR) on Binance, you will want to hover over the tab which is called “Exchange” and then mouse click on “Basic”.

In order to discover Numeraire (NMR), you will need to put the shortname of the coin (NMR) into the search-box, which is marked in the graphic. Be certain of having selected the correct grouping, we’ve marked this too. Click on “NMR/BTC” to go on.

Step 4 – Acquisition of Numeraire (NMR)

After you’ve spotted Numeraire (NMR), you’ll be presented with a Price Chart (in the Center of the picture) and a few Order Books (on the Left of the picture). And now we are going to make our own Limit Order.

The ”Price” field will be filled automatically with the most latest rate Numeraire (NMR) was traded for. This can be changed to your desired rate. The ”Amount” field is the amount of NMR you want to purchase. The field with the “Total” tag is the aggregated price in Bitcoin that you’ll pay for the complete amount of NMR.

If you are happy, click on “Buy NMR”. Your plan will be filled as soon as a Seller trades their NMR for the price you submitted.

Step 5 – Withdraw Numeraire (NMR)

Unless you are frequently trading, never keep your coins on an Crypto Trader for an extended period of time. To take off your Numeraire (NMR), go to the ”Funds” tab and mouse click on “Withdraw”. Numeraire (NMR) can be safely stored using a Wallet, e.g. MyEtherWallet. (MyEtherWallet Setup Guide)

Locate Numeraire (NMR) by using the given drop-down box. Put into the quantity of Numeraire (NMR) you would like to take off. Take into account the shown minimus/fees. Also make sure you have entered your precise withdraw address.

If you are happy, make a mouse click on “Submit”. You could be asked for verfication via email/phone before a withdrawal.

Congratulations! You’ve successfully purchased and stored Numeraire (NMR).

That’s it!

Take offs from Crypto Exchange can durate anywhere from 1 – 30 minutes (rely on network usage). Once you’ve proved your withdrawal with Etherscan.io or Ethplorer.io and the Numeraire (NMR) is stored inside your wallet, you are all done.
