How to Buy Blockstack (STX)

A Step-by-Step-Guide on how to buy Blockstack (STX)

Step 1 – Join Kraken

You will first need to purchase units of the bigger currencies. Exchanges commonly accept either Bitcoin or Ethereum in exchange for alternative Coins. Follow the Link below to register and getting your Bitcoin or Ethereum.

Kraken Signup
Step 2 – Transfer Your Ethereum or Bitcoin to an Crypto Trader

Your Bitcoin (BTC) or Ethereum (ETH) has to be sent over to an Crypto Trader now. Every exchange has a deposit form that is similar. Blockstack (STX) is available on Binance, so we’re going to use that as an sample. Not sure on how to send funds from Coinbase to an Crypto Trader? (Follow these easy steps)

Make sure you’ve chosen the right coin you wish to deposit.

Bitcoin must be sent to a Bitcoin deposit address, ETH to an ETH deposit address, etc. In the case you’re working with a decentralized exchange, such as Airswap or DDEX could be divergent (Follow our guide).

Binance Signup
Step 3 – Find Blockstack (STX)

Once your deposit has been affirmed, you will now have access to your finances and you can initiate purchasing and trading. To purchase Blockstack (STX) on Binance, you will want to hover over the tab which is called “Exchange” and click on “Basic”.

To find Blockstack (STX), you will need to put the cointype (STX) into the search-box, which is marked in the image. Be certain of having choosen the right pairing, we’ve marked this too. Click on “STX/BTC” to proceed.

Step 4 – Buy Blockstack (STX)

After you have spotted Blockstack (STX), you’ll be presented with a Price Chart (in the Center of the picture) and a few Order Books (on the Left of the picture). Next we are going to create our own Limit Order.

The field with the “Price” tag will be filled automatically with the recent valuation Blockstack (STX) was sold for. You can change this to your desired price. The field with the “Amount” tag is the quantity of STX you like to buy. The field with the “Total” tag is the total price in Bitcoin that you will pay for the complete quantity of STX.

When you’re happy, click on “Buy STX”. Your order will be filled as soon as a Vender trades their STX for the price you gave.

Step 5 – Withdraw Blockstack (STX)

Unless you are regularly trading, never keep your crypto on an Crypto Trader for an longer time period. To withdraw your Blockstack (STX), hover over the ”Funds” tab and mouse click on “Withdraw”. Blockstack (STX) can be stored securely using a Wallet, for instance MyEtherWallet. (MyEtherWallet Setup Guide)

Find Blockstack (STX) by using the given drop-down box. Drop in the amount of Blockstack (STX) you would like to withdraw. Don’t forget the displayed minimus/fees. Also make sure you have filled your right withdraw address.

When you are glad, click “Submit”. You may be asked for verfication via email/phone before a withdrawal.

Kudos! You have successfully bought and safed Blockstack (STX).

That’s it!

Withdrawals from Crypto Exchange can take anywhere from 1 – 30 minutes (rely on network usage). As soon as you have proved your take off using Etherscan.io or Ethplorer.io and the Blockstack (STX) is safe inside your wallet, you are all done.
