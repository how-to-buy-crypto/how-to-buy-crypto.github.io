How to Buy Synthetix Network Token (SNX)

A Step-by-Step-Guide on how to buy Synthetix Network Token (SNX)

Step 1 – Join Kraken

You’ll first need to buy one of the top-tier currencies. Exchanges normally accept either ETH (Ethereum) or BTC (Bitcoin) in exchange for any other Cryptocurrency. Follow the Link below for registration and receive your ETH (Ethereum) or BTC (Bitcoin).

Kraken Signup
Step 2 – Send Your Ethereum (ETH) or Bitcoin (BTC) to an Crypto Exchange

Your Bitcoin (BTC) or Ethereum (ETH) will need to be sent over to an Crypto Exchange now. All exchanges have a deposit form that is identical. Synthetix Network Token (SNX) is available on Binance, so we will use that as an example. Not sure on how to send Coinbase funds to an Crypto Exchange? (Just follow this guide)

Make sure you have chosen the precise coin you want to deposit.

BTC must be sent to a BTC deposit address, Ethereum to an Ethereum deposit address, etcetera. If you’re working with a decentralized exchange, like Airswap or DDEX could be unlike (Just follow our manual).

Binance Signup
Step 3 – Locate Synthetix Network Token (SNX)

As soon as your deposit has been verified, you will now have access to your assets and you can commence buying and trading. To buy Synthetix Network Token (SNX) on Binance, you will want to go to the tab which is called “Exchange” and after that click on “Basic”.

To spot Synthetix Network Token (SNX), you will need to insert the shortname of the coin (SNX) into the search box, which is highlighted in the image. Make sure you have selected the correct pairing, we’ve highlighted this too. Click on “SNX/BTC” to progress.

Step 4 – Purchase Synthetix Network Token (SNX)

After you’ve spotted Synthetix Network Token (SNX), you’ll be given a Price Chart (in the Center of the image) and some Order Books (on the Left of the image). Next we’re making our Limit Order.

The ”Price” field will be filled with the latest valuation Synthetix Network Token (SNX) was sold for. This can be changed to your favoured rate. The ”Amount” field is the amount of SNX you like to purchase. The ”Total” field is the aggregated price in Bitcoin that you will settle for the complete amount of SNX.

When you’re satisfied, click on “Buy SNX”. Your order will be filled when a Vendor trades their SNX for the price you introduced.

Step 5 – Withdraw Synthetix Network Token (SNX)

Unless you’re frequently trading, never keep your crypto on an Exchange for an extensive time frame. To withdraw your Synthetix Network Token (SNX), go to the ”Funds” tab and click on “Withdraw”. Synthetix Network Token (SNX) can be stored safely using a Wallet, for instance MyEtherWallet. (MyEtherWallet Setup Guide)

Find Synthetix Network Token (SNX) by using the given drop-down box. Drop in the amount of Synthetix Network Token (SNX) you would wish to withdraw. Don’t forget the shown minimus/fees. Also make sure you have filled your precise withdraw address.

If you’re satisfied, click “Submit”. You could be asked for email/phone verification ahead of a take off.

Kudos! You’ve successfully purchased and safed Synthetix Network Token (SNX).

That’s it!

Withdrawals from Crypto Exchange can durate anywhere from 1 – 30 minutes (depending on network usage). Once you’ve proved your withdrawal using Etherscan.io or Ethplorer.io and the Synthetix Network Token (SNX) is safe within your wallet, you’re done.
