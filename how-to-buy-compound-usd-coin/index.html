How to Buy cUSDC (CUSDC)

A Step-by-Step-Guide on how to buy cUSDC (CUSDC)

Step 1 – Join Kraken

In the beginning you’ll need to buy one of the major currencies. Exchanges commonly accept either ETH (Ethereum) or BTC (Bitcoin) in exchange for alternative Coins. Click the button below for registration and getting your ETH (Ethereum) or BTC (Bitcoin).

Kraken Signup
Step 2 – Send Your Funds to an Cryptocurrency Exchange

Your ETH (Ethereum) or BTC (Bitcoin) will need to be sent over to an Cryptocurrency Exchange now. All exchanges have a deposit form that is identical. cUSDC (CUSDC) is available on Binance, so we will use that as an sample. Not sure on how to send Coinbase funds to an Cryptocurrency Exchange? (Follow these easy steps)

Make sure you have chosen the correct coin you want to deposit.

BTC must be sent to a BTC deposit address, ETH to an ETH deposit address, et cetera. In the case you are dealing with a decentralized exchange, like Airswap or DDEX could be different (Follow our manual).

Binance Signup
Step 3 – Locate cUSDC (CUSDC)

Once your deposit has been confirmed, you will instantly have access to your funds and you can begin invest and trading. To purchase cUSDC (CUSDC) on Binance, you will want to hover over the tab which is called “Exchange” and then click on “Basic”.

To find cUSDC (CUSDC), you will need to put the cointype (CUSDC) into the search box, which is marked in the picture. Be certain of having selected the proper pairing, we’ve marked this too. Click on “CUSDC/BTC” to go on.

Step 4 – Acquisition of cUSDC (CUSDC)

Once you have located cUSDC (CUSDC), you’ll be presented with a Chart with Prices (in the Center of the picture) and some Order Books (Leftside of the picture). Next we are going to create our Limit Order.

The ”Price” field will be automatically filled with the up-to-date tariff cUSDC (CUSDC) was traded for. This can be changed to your preferred price. The field with the “Amount” tag is the amount of CUSDC you wish to purchase. The field with the “Total” tag is the aggregated price in BTC that you will settle for the whole amount of CUSDC.

When you’re happy, make a mouse click on “Buy CUSDC”. Your order will be filled when a Seller sells their CUSDC for the price you introduced.

Step 5 – Withdraw cUSDC (CUSDC)

Unless you’re frequently trading, best advise is not to keep your coins on an Crypto Trader for an longer time frame. In order to withdraw your cUSDC (CUSDC), go to the tab called “Funds” and mouse click on “Withdraw”. cUSDC (CUSDC) can be stored securely using a Wallet, for instance MyEtherWallet. (MyEtherWallet Setup Guide)

Find cUSDC (CUSDC) by using the provided drop-down box. Put into the amount of cUSDC (CUSDC) you would wish to withdraw. Don’t forget the shown fees/minimums. Also make sure you have entered your precise withdraw address.

If you’re happy, click on “Submit”. You may be requested for phone/email verification before a take off.

Congrats! You have successfully bought and safed cUSDC (CUSDC).

That’s it!

Take offs from Crypto Trader can take anywhere from 1 – 30 minutes (rely on network usage). As soon as you’ve proved your take off with Etherscan.io or Ethplorer.io and the cUSDC (CUSDC) is stored within your wallet, you’re finished.
