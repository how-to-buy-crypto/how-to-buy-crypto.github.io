How to Buy Aragon (ANT)

A Step-by-Step-Guide on how to buy Aragon (ANT)

Step 1 – Signup to Kraken

You will first need to purchase units of the bigger coins. Exchanges usually accept either Ethereum or Bitcoin in exchange for Altcoins. Click the button below to register and receive your Ethereum or Bitcoin.

Kraken Signup
Step 2 – Transmit Your Funds to an Crypto Trader

Your Bitcoin or Ethereum will need to be sent over to an Crypto Trader now. Every exchange has a deposit form that is similar. Aragon (ANT) is available on Binance, so we’re going to use that as an example. Unsure on how to send Coinbase funds to an Crypto Trader? (Follow this guide)

Make sure you’ve chosen the right coin you would like to deposit.

BTC must be sent to a BTC deposit address, ETH to an ETH deposit address, etcetera. In the case you are dealing with a decentralized exchange, like Airswap or DDEX could be opposed (Follow our guide).

Binance Signup
Step 3 – Locate Aragon (ANT)

As soon as your deposit has been approved, you will instantly have access to your assets and you can initiate invest and trading. To purchase Aragon (ANT) on Binance, you will want to go to the tab which is called “Exchange” and then click on “Basic”.

To find Aragon (ANT), you will need to insert the short name of the asset (ANT) into the search-box, which is marked in the graphic. Make sure you have choosen the correct grouping, we’ve marked this too. Click on “ANT/BTC” to progress.

Step 4 – Buying Aragon (ANT)

After you have found Aragon (ANT), you will be given a Price Chart (in the Center of the picture) and a few Order Books (Leftside of the picture). For now we’re creating our Limit Order.

The field with the “Price” tag will be filled automatically with the recent price Aragon (ANT) was sold for. This can be changed to your favoured price. The ”Amount” field is the volumen of ANT you want to purchase. The field with the “Total” tag is the total price in Bitcoin that you will settle for the complete volumen of ANT.

If you are glad, make a mouse click on “Buy ANT”. Your order will be filled when a Vender trades their ANT for the price you submitted.

Step 5 – Withdraw Aragon (ANT)

Unless you’re frequently trading, best advise is not to keep your crypto on an Exchange for an extensive time frame. In order to take off your Aragon (ANT), go to the tab called “Funds” and click on “Withdraw”. Aragon (ANT) can be safely stored using a Wallet, for instance MyEtherWallet. (MyEtherWallet Setup Guide)

Find Aragon (ANT) by using the given drop-down box. Put into the quantity of Aragon (ANT) you’d wish to take off. Take into account the displayed minimus/fees. Also make sure you have filled your precise withdraw address.

When you’re happy, click “Submit”. You may be requested for verfication via phone/email ahead of a withdrawal.

Congratulations! You’ve successfully bought and safed Aragon (ANT).

That’s it!

Take offs from Crypto Exchange can durate anywhere from 1 – 30 minutes (depending on network usage). As soon as you’ve confirmed your take off using Etherscan.io or Ethplorer.io and the Aragon (ANT) is stored in your wallet, you are done.
