How to Buy Terra (LUNA)

A Step-by-Step-Guide on how to buy Terra (LUNA)

Step 1 – Join Kraken

You’ll first need to purchase one of the bigger currencies. Exchanges commonly accept either Bitcoin (BTC) or Ethereum (ETH) in exchange for alternative Coins. Click below to register and to get your Bitcoin (BTC) or Ethereum (ETH).

Kraken Signup
Step 2 – Transfer Your Funds to an Crypto Exchange

Your ETH (Ethereum) or BTC (Bitcoin) has to be sent over to an Crypto Exchange now. Every exchange has a deposit form that is identical. Terra (LUNA) is accessible on Binance, so we’re going to use that as an example. Not sure on how to send funds from Coinbase to an Crypto Exchange? (Follow this how-to)

Make sure you’ve chosen the precise coin you want to deposit.

BTC must be sent to a BTC deposit address, Ethereum to an Ethereum deposit address, etcetera. In the case you’re working with a decentralized exchange, such as Airswap or DDEX could be different (Follow our how-to).

Binance Signup
Step 3 – Find Terra (LUNA)

Once your deposit has been affirmed, you’ll instantly have access to your assets and you can begin invest and trading. To purchase Terra (LUNA) on Binance, you’ll want to hover over the tab which is called “Exchange” and after that mouse click on “Basic”.

In order to find Terra (LUNA), you’ll need to put the shortname of the coin (LUNA) into the search box, which is highlighted in the image. Be certain of having selected the correct pairing, we have highlighted this also. Click on “LUNA/BTC” to progress.

Step 4 – Purchase Terra (LUNA)

As soon as you have located Terra (LUNA), you’ll be given a Price Chart (in the Center of the image) and some Order Books (Leftside of the image). And now we’re going to create our Limit Order.

The ”Price” field will be automatically filled with the most up-to-date rate Terra (LUNA) was sold for. You can change this to your favoured price. The ”Amount” field is the quantity of LUNA you want to purchase. The ”Total” field is the whole price in BTC that you’ll settle for the whole quantity of LUNA.

When you are satisfied, make a mouse click on “Buy LUNA”. Your order will be filled as soon as a Vendor sells their LUNA for the price you offered.

Step 5 – Withdraw Terra (LUNA)

Unless you’re frequently trading, best advise is not to keep your crypto coins on an Crypto Exchange for an extended time frame. To withdraw your Terra (LUNA), go to the ”Funds” tab and mouse click on “Withdraw”. Terra (LUNA) can be stored safely using a Wallet, for example MyEtherWallet. (MyEtherWallet Setup Guide)

Find Terra (LUNA) by using the given drop-down box. Enter the quantity of Terra (LUNA) you would wish to withdraw. Don’t forget the demonstrated minimus/fees. Also make sure you have entered your correct withdraw address.

If you’re happy, make a mouse click on “Submit”. You may be requested for verfication via phone/email ahead of a take off.

Kudos! You’ve successfully purchased and safed Terra (LUNA).

That’s it!

Take offs from Cryptocurrency Exchange can durate anywhere from 1 – 30 minutes (rely on network usage). As soon as you’ve confirmed your withdrawal using Etherscan.io or Ethplorer.io and the Terra (LUNA) is stored within your wallet, you’re all done.
